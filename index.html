🌍 SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

⚙️ What are the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)?

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are 17 global objectives set in 2015 to be achieved by 2030. They aim to tackle major global challenges like poverty, inequality, climate change, education, and health! They are for **everyone, everywhere**!

❤️ What is SDG 3?

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being ensures healthy lives and promotes well-being for people of all ages. It’s about **ending preventable deaths**, improving mental and physical health, and making sure **healthcare is available to all**! Health is a human right — not a privilege.

🎯 Key Targets of SDG 3

  • Reduce global maternal mortality below 70 per 100,000 live births
  • End epidemics like AIDS, malaria, tuberculosis & neglected tropical diseases
  • Reduce deaths from noncommunicable diseases (like cancer, diabetes, heart disease)
  • Improve mental health and well-being
  • Reduce road injuries & deaths
  • Universal health coverage for all
  • Access to safe, effective, affordable medicines & vaccines
  • Health education & awareness worldwide

🧠 Mental Health Matters

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Disorders like depression, anxiety, PTSD, and stress-related illnesses affect millions, especially youth. SDG 3 promotes support systems, counseling, and ending stigma around mental illness. It’s okay to not be okay — and it’s okay to ask for help.

🌎 Why SDG 3 Matters to the World

- Every year, 5 million children under 5 die of preventable causes. - Over 1 billion people lack access to basic healthcare. - Noncommunicable diseases cause 70% of all deaths. - 1 in 4 people will experience mental illness in their lifetime.

A healthy world is a productive world. SDG 3 helps every country move towards equity, peace, and prosperity.

  • Talk openly about mental health 🧠
  • Support vaccination campaigns 💉
  • Volunteer or donate to health-related charities 🤝
  • Educate yourself and others about first aid & health emergencies 🚑
  • Use roads safely & wear seatbelts 🚗
  • Encourage clean environments to prevent disease 🌿
